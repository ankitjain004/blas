How this is organized

The official takeUforward SDE Sheet has ~27 topic-sections (Arrays I-IV, LL I-II, Greedy, Recursion, R&B, BS, Heaps, S&Q I-II, String I-II, BT I-III, BST I-II, BT-Misc, Graph I-II, DP I-II, Trie). Each section has 6–7 problems.

Instead of marching topic-by-topic (the standard 18-day plan), this series picks question N from every topic on day N. By Day 6 most topics are exhausted; Day 7 catches the 12 topics that have a 7th question.

Total: 6 × 27 + 25 = 187 cards — every problem on the full Ankit's SDE Sheet.
